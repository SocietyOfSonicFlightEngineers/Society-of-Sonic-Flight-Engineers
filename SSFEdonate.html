How do I donate?

Step 1

First, click on the above "Donate" button to open https://www.txamfoundation.com/give.aspx and press the “Search for an Account” button

Step 2

In the “Search Accounts” menu, input any string until the “Can’t find the account you’re looking for?” field pops up. There, you can enter our account number: 944280, and hit enter

Step 3

Then, enter an amount you’d like to donate. You can choose many options for how the donation is processed, such as whether you’re donating as an individual or institution, as part of a prior pledge, and if your employer will match your donation. It is highly recommended to check whether your employer offers donation matching.
