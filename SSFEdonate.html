How do I donate?

Step 1

First, open https://myaggieland.txamfoundation.com/tamf/give and choose gift type and amount.

Step 2

Select “Student Organization Gifts” (500-99990) and enter the account number 944280 into the Gift Notes section

Step 3

You may choose additional options for processing the gift, including employer donation matching and marking it as a tribute. It is highly recommended to check whether your employer offers donation matching.

Note: Gifts made through the Texas A&M Foundation are tax deductible to the extent permitted by law.
